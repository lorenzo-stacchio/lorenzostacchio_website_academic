About

I am a Research Fellow (Assegnista di Ricerca, SSD IINF-05/A — Information Processing Systems) at the VRAI Lab, University of Macerata, under the supervision of Prof. Emanuele Frontoni. My current research focuses on consumer behaviour analysis in retail through top-view RGB-D cameras.

I obtained a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Bologna in 2024 with the thesis Entangling artificial intelligence with extended reality paradigms for human activity support (Advisor: Prof. Gustavo Marfia, Co-Advisor: Prof. Giuseppe Lisanti). I am Associate Editor of Springer's The Visual Computer and recipient of the Springer Nature Editor of Distinction Award 2026.

Research interests

Computer Vision & Generative AI for XR

Deep Learning for multimodal object & scene understanding; Multimodal LLMs, Large Reconstruction Models and Neural Rendering for context-aware 3D content generation in immersive environments.

Human–machine interaction in XR

Cognitive & perceptual modelling of user behaviour, attentional regulation, sense of presence; Digital Twins and multisensory environments for adaptive, personalised experiences.

Extended Artificial Intelligence (XRAI)

Frameworks bridging perception and generation across XR pipelines, with applications to creative industries, cultural heritage, manufacturing, education and retail.

AI ethics

Risk management frameworks for bias, privacy, transparency and explainability in joint AI + XR systems — toward responsible, sustainable deployments.
