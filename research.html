Research profile

My research focuses on the intersection between Extended Reality (XR) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), with the goal of developing innovative solutions that support human activity across interdisciplinary contexts. Work spans immersive experiences in Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR) and Mixed Reality (MR); the design of advanced Machine Learning and Deep Learning architectures; and their integration within unified Extended Artificial Intelligence (XRAI) frameworks.

Research is currently carried out at the VRAI Lab (directed by Prof. Primo Zingaretti and Prof. Emanuele Frontoni) within the SPOCRI Department, University of Macerata. Long-standing collaborations remain active with the VARLAB (Prof. Gustavo Marfia) at the University of Bologna and with the CFC International Research Center.

Theoretical contributions

Vision & multimodal recognition

Supervised & unsupervised Deep Learning for object/scene classification and multimodal identification — including augmented wine recognition and socio-historical analysis of vernacular photography.

Generative AI for XR

Systematisation of Multimodal LLMs, Large Reconstruction Models and Neural Rendering to produce context-aware 3D content. See MineVRA (Best Paper, IEEE VR 2025) and X-NR.

Digital Twins & collaboration

XR frameworks to annotate, visualise and manipulate Digital Twins — and to study procedural learning (e.g., sequential instruction learning with the Rubik's cube).

Ethics of XRAI

XRAI-Ethics: a framework for managing risks (bias, privacy, transparency, explainability) at the intersection of AI and XR.

Aesthetic & emotional perception

Evaluation of aesthetic emotions and social presence in immersive musical experiences (live vs. 2D vs. 3D recorded concerts).

Applied research

Creative Industries

Immersive x-commerce, Human Digital Twins for personalised retail interactions, generative AI in XR collaborative environments (CreAIXR).

Cultural Heritage

The IMAGO dataset (80k+ photos, 1845–2009), AR-based family-album digitisation on Hololens 2, and DanXe — an XRAI framework for dance heritage.

Industry & manufacturing

Annholotator, an MR collaborative platform for Lean Manufacturing work instructions; AR systems for wine-label recognition; modular XR flight simulators (Unity-VRLines).

Retail analytics

Consumer behaviour analysis in physical retail using top-view RGB-D cameras — the focus of current research at UNIMC.
